The Bond of Friendship


Friendship's bond, a treasure rare,
A connection deep, beyond compare.
Through laughter and tears, we share our hearts,
And in each other, we find our parts.

In times of need, we lend a hand,
And stand together, on shifting sand.
We celebrate life's joys and wins,
And lift each other, through troubled skins.

Through friendship's love, we find our way,
To a sense of belonging, every day.
A bond so strong, it stands the test,
Of time and distance, and life's unrest.
