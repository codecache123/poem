The Beauty of Nature


Nature's canvas stretches far and wide,
A masterpiece of colors, side by side.
The sun rises high in morning's glow,
Painting the sky with hues of gold.

The trees stand tall, their leaves rustling free,
A gentle breeze whispers secrets to me.
The flowers bloom, their beauty on display,
A vibrant tapestry, in every way.

In nature's arms, we find our peace,
A sense of calm, our worries cease.
The world's noise fades, as we behold,
The beauty that surrounds us, young and old.
