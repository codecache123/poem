Life's Journey


Life's journey winds its twisted way,
Through hills and valleys, night and day.
We walk, we run, we stumble and fall,
But rise again, to give our all.

With every step, a memory made,
A lesson learned, a path displayed.
We find our strength, our guiding light,
To navigate life's darkest night.

Through trials and tribulations we grow,
And wisdom's lessons, we begin to know.
With each step forward, we find our way,
To a brighter dawn, a new day.
