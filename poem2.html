The Power of Hope


Hope's flame burns bright, a guiding light,
In darkness's depths, it shines so bright.
A beacon in the stormy night,
It leads us through, to morning's light.

With every breath, hope's voice whispers low,
"You are strong, you can make it go."
It lifts our hearts, it soothes our soul,
And gives us courage, to make us whole.

Through life's ups and downs, hope stands tall,
A steadfast friend, that never will fall.
It reminds us, that better days,
Are waiting ahead, in brighter ways
